Firebuzz

Firebuzz is a leading edge wildfire detection platform. It can be used by local authorities to enhance communication to the public, it can be used by the public to communicate locations of wildfires to authorities and it can be used by research institutes to acquire information about wildfires in real-time.

This application was developed for the Space Apps Challenge 2018 from NASA.
